Residential Electrical

Welcome to our residential electrical service.

At Pepe Electric LLC, we are committed to providing safe and efficient electrical solutions for your home.

Our team of expert electricians is here to help you keep your home running safely and efficiently.

Why choose us?

Experience: With years of experience in the electrical industry, our team has the necessary knowledge to address a wide range of residential electrical issues.
Professionalism: We take pride in offering professional and reliable service to all our customers.
Safety: Safety is our top priority. We ensure that all work meets the strictest safety standards.
Energy Efficiency: We help our customers reduce their energy consumption and save on long-term costs by implementing energy-efficient solutions.

Services:
Installation and maintenance of residential electrical systems.
Troubleshooting electrical faults.
Upgrading old electrical systems.
Installation of interior and exterior lighting.
Energy efficiency consulting.
Installation of renewable energy systems.

Commitment to Safety:

We conduct thorough inspections to identify potential electrical hazards in your home.
We use high-quality equipment and materials that meet safety regulations.
Our electricians are trained in safe work practices and strictly follow safety protocols.

Energy Efficiency:

We offer customized solutions to reduce your home's energy consumption.
We install energy-saving devices, such as smart thermostats and LED lights, to help you save on your electricity bills.
We provide advice on the best ways to optimize energy use in your home.
