About Us:

History

Pepe Electric LLC is a locally owned commercial and residential electrician company serving Connecticut, Fairfield County. All of the electricians on our staff are certified, licensed and insured. For over a decade we’ve been proudly serving these community, forming a unique connection with each client, building & maintaining their trust. These trusting relationships have allowed our business to grow through referral clients and expand beyond the Connecticut and New York neighborhoods. At Pepe Electric LLC we pride ourselves on exceptional customer service, respect and honesty with both our work and our pricing.

Commercial & Residential Electrician Services

Our licensed and certified electrician technicians are trained to repair, maintain and update a variety of electric components in your home or commercial property. From electrical repairs to code violations, electric panel upgrades, lighting installation, emergency power restoration and more, we guarantee our work and your satisfaction. When diagnosing and repairing electrical problems in your home or business you need a company you can trust to get the job right the first time. Pepe Electric LLC offers 100% satisfaction guarantee on all of our work, from wiring a circuit breaker to electrical switch repairs and installing a ceiling fan. We pride ourselves on accountability and responsibility.
